Hi, I'm Heidi. Here's a bit about me...

With over two decades of experience in garden design and a passion for creating harmonious outdoor spaces, I bring a unique blend of artistic vision and horticultural expertise to every project. My journey began with formal training at the Royal Horticultural Society, where I developed a deep understanding of plant communities, sustainable design practices, and the technical aspects of landscape architecture.

My design philosophy centers on creating gardens that feel both timeless and contemporary—spaces that honor the architectural context of your home while introducing elements that reflect your personal style and practical needs. I believe the most successful gardens establish a meaningful dialogue between built structures and natural elements, creating a seamless transition that enhances both.

Each project begins with careful listening and observation. Understanding how you wish to use your garden, the existing site conditions, and the surrounding landscape allows me to craft spaces that not only look beautiful but function perfectly for your lifestyle. Whether you're seeking a tranquil retreat, a vibrant entertainment area, or a productive kitchen garden, I'll work closely with you to transform your vision into reality.

My work has been recognized with multiple awards at prestigious garden shows and featured in leading design publications. However, my greatest satisfaction comes from creating gardens that bring joy to their owners—spaces that evolve beautifully through the seasons and provide a lasting connection to nature. I look forward to discussing how we might collaborate to create your perfect outdoor space.
